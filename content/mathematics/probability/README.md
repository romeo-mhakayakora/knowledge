# Probability Theory

Foundation of probability, random variables, distributions, and fundamental theorems.

## Overview

This subject provides the mathematical foundations for understanding randomness, uncertainty, and probabilistic reasoning.

## Chapter Structure

### Chapter 1: Fundamentals
Basic probability concepts, sample spaces, events, conditional probability, and Bayes' theorem.

### Chapter 2: Random Variables
Discrete and continuous random variables, expectation, variance, and important distributions.

## Learning Outcomes

By studying this subject you will:
- Understand probability spaces and axiomatic definitions
- Master conditional probability and independence
- Work fluently with random variables
- Apply Bayes' theorem and fundamental theorems
- Recognize and work with common distributions

## Prerequisites

- Basic algebra
- Familiarity with sets and logic

## Connections

- **To Statistics:** Foundation for statistical inference
- **To AI/ML:** Essential for probabilistic models
- **To Physics:** Quantum mechanics foundations
